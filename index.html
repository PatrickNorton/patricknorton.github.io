Research

I am interested in cryptography, zero-knowledge proofs, complexity theory, discrete mathematics, Lie theory, and machine learning. My undergraduate thesis was on zero-knowledge probabilistically-checkable proofs and their variants.

With Olive, I am currently working on methods to allow auditors to verify that AI/ML systems are trustworthy and preserve fairness. In the past, I have done work at Los Alamos working on the DIORAMA project, including writing the femtotime library for high-precision time tracking in C++.
